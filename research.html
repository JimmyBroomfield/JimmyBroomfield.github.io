Research Interests

I am interested in many areas of math, but I am currently working to find a specialization. A few of the options that I am considering are partial differential equation, differential geometry, and numerical analysis. These fields appeal to me because they can be applied to important physical problems. The blocks below give a snippet of what I find attractive about math, physics, and computation.

Mathematics

I enjoy many areas of pure mathematics. Some of these include analysis, algebra, differential geometry, and topology.

Physics

I find several areas of physics exciting. I would like to learn more in the areas of quantum mechanics, quantum field theory, and general relativity.

Computation

Numerical methods are important in solving many real world problems. I hope to continue learning techniques in numerical analysis.
